# EDF Energy Compliance Playbook

## Known aliases
- EDF, EDF Energy, Électricité de France

## Supplier-specific rules

1. **Smart/AMR meter warnings.** Customer must explicitly confirm they
   are "happy to submit readings" or equivalent. Agent mentioning it
   without customer agreement is `partial`.

2. **Multi-metered sites.** Customer must confirm understanding and
   agreement with single-meter billing arrangement.

3. **Third-party disclosure.** Agent must state "not EDF" clearly early
   in the call.

## Customer confirmation

Strict for EDF — trailing "mm" alone does NOT count. Needs:
"yes", "yeah", "yep", "okay", "right", "sure", "happy with that",
"happy to", "that's fine".

Silence or trailing-off is explicitly NOT confirmation for EDF.
