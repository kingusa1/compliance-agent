For Business Tariff Information - MUST BE READ VERBATIM  
Our For Business tariff consists of two price components . The first part is a fixed price element, 
the majority of which is for the energy we supply you, so you're protected from rising wholesale 
energy prices.  The second part is variable cost component, covering industry charges each 
quarter such as network, social, and environmental obligations.  These costs may increase or 
decrease throughout the tariff term. If these variable costs change, your charges will be updated 
accordingly every quarter on 1st Jan uary, 1st April, 1st July and 1st October until the end of your 
tariff, and these new charges will apply until the next quarterly update.
